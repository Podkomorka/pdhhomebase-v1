To include a card image in your article use the following syntax: [[Soulherder]]. Articles should not include more than about 6 - 10 card images so use them wisely.

Article Length

Articles should keep to a max word count of about 1,500.

Links

If you want to make some text into a link use the following syntax directly in your article:

<a href="URL to destination" target="_blank">text that you click</a>

Example:

Thank you for reading my article. Before you leave remeber to visit <a href="https://www.pdhhomebase.com/" target="_blank">The PDH Home Base</a> to check out other articles.

Linking a Card

If you link to a card image only use Scryfalls card image URLs.
